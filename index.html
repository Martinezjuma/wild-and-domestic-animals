    Dogs

    Domestic dogs are mostly kept as pets,though many breeds are capable of surviving on their own, whether it's in a forest or on city streets.

    Cats

    Domestic cats are characterized by supple low-slung bodies, finely molded heads,long tails that aid in balance,and specialized teeth and claws that adapt them admirably to a life of active hunting.

    Sheep

    Sheep or domestic sheep are domesticated,ruminant mammals typically kept as livestock.
